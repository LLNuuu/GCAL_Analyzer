GCAL Analyzer

GCAL Analyzer is a Chrome extension that visualizes and analyzes time spent across your Google Calendar events — with charts, breakdowns, and percentages — right in your browser's side panel.

Features

Quick date presets, stacked bar charts, per-calendar and per-event breakdowns with hours and percentages, expand/collapse details, and calendar toggle — all powered by read-only access to your existing Google Calendar data.

Privacy

Your calendar data is processed locally in your browser and is never sent to any third-party server. Read our full Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
