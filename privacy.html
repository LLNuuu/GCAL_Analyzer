Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Overview

GCAL Analyzer is a Chrome extension that visualizes and analyzes time spent across your Google Calendar events. Your privacy is important to us.

Data collection

GCAL Analyzer accesses event titles, start/end times, and calendar metadata (name, color) via the Google Calendar API with read-only access.

Data usage

Your calendar data is used solely to generate charts and time breakdowns within the extension's side panel. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Data storage

GCAL Analyzer does not store your calendar data persistently. Data is fetched on demand and exists only in memory during your active session. No cookies, local storage, or databases are used.

Data sharing

Your data is never sent to any third-party server, analytics service, or external API. Your data is never sold, shared, or transferred to any third party. The only external communication is between your browser and Google's Calendar API.

Permissions

sidePanel: displays the extension interface. identity: authenticates with Google via Chrome's built-in OAuth.

Google API Services

GCAL Analyzer's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
